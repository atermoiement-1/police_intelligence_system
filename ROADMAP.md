# AIPAS Development Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the planned future milestones and enhancements for the AI Police Assistance System.

## Phase 4: Smart Cities & IoT
*   [ ] **ANPR Integration**: Automate license plate extraction from public camera streams.
*   [ ] **Geospatial Real-time Alerters**: Visual sirens highlighting active SOS reports.
*   [ ] **Private Blockchain Ledger**: Immutable evidence chain of custody transfers.

## Phase 5: Voice & Speech NLP
*   [ ] **Speech-to-Text Transcribers**: Voice statement recorders converting speech directly to written case diaries.
*   [ ] **Multi-Lingual Translation**: Expand RAG advice models to support regional languages.
