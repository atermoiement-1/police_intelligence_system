# Changelog

All notable changes to the AI Police Assistance System (AIPAS) will be documented in this file.

## [v3.0.0] - 2026-07-18
### Added
*   **Court & Prosecution Center**: Track schedules, prosecutor assignments, and bail statuses.
*   **Document Management Center**: Admissibility checks with Magistrate digital signature validation.
*   **Telemetry & Compliance Logs**: Heartbeats telemetry, immutable logs database, and CSV export.
*   **Citizen Complaint Sync**: Real-time synchronization of citizen submissions to the officer's intake queue.
*   **Facial Biometrics**: Active login scans and trusted devices session managers.

## [v2.0.0] - 2026-07-17
### Added
*   Digital Evidence Hub with automated SHA-256 file hashing.
*   Interactive SVG Relationship Graphs mapping crime connections.
*   LangGraph-powered BNS section recommendation and classification node.
